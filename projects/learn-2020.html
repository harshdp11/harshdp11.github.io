RoundGlass Learn Summit

The RoundGlass Learning Summit held in Jaisalmer aims at bringing together the world’s most advanced thinkers and doers in the fields of learning and wellbeing, to collectively inspire, inform and create the learning ecosystems of the future.

The design and engineering teams were tasked with creating an app that caters to the experience of hosts and attendees before, during and, after the event with the overall objective being to build a community of like-minded people who think more holistic about education and learning.

Key App Functionalities

The app should have features that help the users in :

Accessing Event Information

Users can access all the necessary event information such as event schedule, venue details, travel, etc before and during the event.

Registering for Sessions

Users can register for sessions they are interested in attending by adding them to their agenda.

Creating Agenda

Users can create their agenda and get reminders for their sessions so they remain on top of their schedule during the event.

Networking

Users can find all the attendees and send an invitation to connect with people they’re interested in. They can engage with fellow attendees in public feed or private chats.

Accessing Resources

Users can access all the useful resources, presentations, study, and reference material.

The Learn Summit 2020 App

Home

All the important event-related information (program, agenda, attendees, venue information, etc) can be accessed from here.

Discussions

Users can share thoughts, images, videos, links, and resources or engage in discussions with the rest of the community.

Messages

Users can send private messages to people in their network.

Notifications

Users receive notifications for upcoming sessions, invite for connection, feedback requests for the session they attended, and much more.

Profile

Users can set up their profiles to showcase their expertise and connect with fellow attendees by connecting with them.
