RoundGlass Platform

RoundGlass is a socially conscious, global organization. RoundGlass aims at reimagining the journey of wellbeing and meaningful living through interactive experiences, cutting-edge applications, and captivating content. Their mission is simple, yet ambitious: to create a vibrant wellness ecosystem by investing in new technology, sharing knowledge, and ensuring that human beings are always at the very center of our approach.

The current RoundGlass corporate website

RoundGlass Collective platform audit

RoundGlass Collective is a platform for individuals to showcase their passion and skills in a visually differentiated and aesthetic manner, engage with like-minded individuals, and grow together. The platform offers modular and rich functionality along with the capability to curate content that can be offered to communities as per their needs.

RoundGlass Collective as a platform can be used to set up a community online offering a set of tools and features configured for their specific needs. While the platform aims at meeting all the community building and management needs within various groups within RoundGlass, it eventually becomes an end to end community-building software with a rich feature library for individuals and corporates alike. The feature focus areas are Community, Collaboration, Content, Curation, and Commerce.
