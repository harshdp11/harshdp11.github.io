RoundGlass Sustain

RoundGlass Sustain is a media-rich resource on India’s natural world. It seeks to create a comprehensive record of the species and ecosystems of the country. As a treasury of wildlife images, videos, and information it will tell stories of India’s biodiversity, the creatures that live within it, and the habitats that support them.

This platform allows people to learn about and discover the magnificence and variety of nature in their own backyards. By creating a globally accessible storehouse of information, RoundGlass Sustain hopes to promote greater respect and affection for all things wild. In showcasing why the environment and all living species are so special, this venture will encourage people to act to protect all of Earth's wealth and diversity. This endeavour to spread knowledge and public awareness is seen as a step toward increasing conservation of the natural world, promoting sustainability, and arresting the decline of species and wild habitats.

About the Project

I had the opportunity to closely work with the sustain team in identifying their key requirements and imagining a scalable and long lasting design that fits perfectly with the roundglass brand. Our goals for the project were :

1. Introduce the new RoundGlass brandiing elements to the existing website
2. Induce user delight through strong visual imagery
3. Provide seamless content discovery and consumption experience across all devices (from desktop to mobile).
